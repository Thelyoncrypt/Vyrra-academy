# Open-Weights: Modified MIT License, Self-Hosting, Deployment

> Kimi K2.6 weights are available on Hugging Face under a Modified MIT License.

## Lesson

Kimi K2.6 weights are available on Hugging Face under a Modified MIT License. The modification: companies exceeding 100 million monthly active users OR generating >$20M USD monthly revenue must prominently display "Kimi K2" branding. "Most teams, including well-funded startups, sit well below both limits".

Self-Hosting Process: Download weights from Hugging Face (moonshotai/Kimi-K2.6); use existing K2.5 deployment configs (architecture is identical, swap weights); configure INT4 quantization; deploy on minimum 4x H100 or recommended 8x H100/H200. Official inference engines: vLLM 0.19.1 (recommended, stable production), SGLang, and KTransformers (Moonshot's own engine).

## Practice

### Self-Hosted Deployment

Self-Hosted Deployment: Deploy Kimi K2.6 with vLLM on a single H100 using INT4 quantization. Benchmark inference latency and throughput across 100 requests. Compare output quality against the API version using a standard eval dataset.
