## Lesson

Deployment Checklist: - Test on physical devices, not just simulators - Optimize AI model sizes through quantization (INT8, INT4) - Implement on-device inference as primary path with cloud fallback - Include privacy nutrition labels for AI data usage - Prepare app store screenshots demonstrating AI features - Budget for app review delays (7-14 days for AI-powered apps)
