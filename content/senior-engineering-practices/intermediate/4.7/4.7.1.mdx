# Requirements Gathering with AI Assistance

> AI tools accelerate requirements gathering by: transcribing stakeholder interviews and extracting key requirements; generating interview question templates for different stakeholder types; analyzing existing documentatio

## Lesson

AI tools accelerate requirements gathering by: transcribing stakeholder interviews and extracting key requirements; generating interview question templates for different stakeholder types; analyzing existing documentation for gaps and inconsistencies; and creating requirement traceability matrices.

Best practice: AI assists but does not replace stakeholder conversations. Always validate AI-generated requirements with domain experts. Requirements risks - misunderstanding, inadequate user involvement, uncertain scope - remain the most central risks in software projects.
