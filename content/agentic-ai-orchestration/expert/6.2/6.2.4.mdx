# Reward Engineering for Agent Behavior Optimization

> Reward engineering shapes agent behavior through carefully designed reward functions.

## Lesson

Reward engineering shapes agent behavior through carefully designed reward functions. The Kimi PARL approach provides a template: R = r_perf + lambda1 * r_parallel + lambda2 * r_finish. Key principles include: (1) make the primary reward dominant by weighting task performance highest; (2) anneal auxiliary rewards to zero during training so the final policy optimizes task quality rather than exploiting auxiliary incentives; (3) design rewards that are dense (frequent feedback) rather than sparse (only at task completion); (4) include penalty terms for undesirable behaviors (repetition, tool misuse, excessive token consumption).
