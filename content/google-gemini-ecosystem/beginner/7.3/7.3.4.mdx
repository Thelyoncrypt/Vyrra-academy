## Lesson

On March 10, 2026, Google embedded Gemini directly into creation workflows across Docs, Sheets, Slides, and Drive: "Help me create" in Docs produces full first drafts from plain language descriptions; "Match writing style" analyzes tone across multi-author documents; "Fill with Gemini" in Sheets achieves 70.48% success rate on complex spreadsheet tasks (9x faster than manual entry for 100-cell tasks); and "Ask Gemini in Drive" enables multi-document simultaneous querying.

Workspace Studio (launched late 2025, Gems integration April 2026) provides no-code automation with Trigger > Reasoning (Gemini) > Action flows. Triggers include new email, spreadsheet row, calendar event, scheduled time, or manual run. Actions include read/send emails, create/update docs/sheets, create calendar events, and run Gemini Gems with Drive knowledge files. Available on Business Standard, Business Plus, Enterprise, and Education Plus at no extra cost.

Data Privacy: Google states Workspace data is not used for model training. Enterprise data residency guarantees, admin-controlled deployment per organizational unit, and audit logging of every agent action in Admin Console.

## Practice

### Workspace Automation

Workspace Automation: Build a Workspace Studio flow that triggers on new sales emails, uses a Gem with product knowledge files to draft personalized responses, and updates a tracking spreadsheet. Measure response quality and time savings over 20 test emails.
