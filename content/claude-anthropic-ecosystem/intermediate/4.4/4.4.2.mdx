# Claude Projects: Knowledge Base, Memory, and Team Features

> Claude Projects are persistent workspaces on Pro ($20/month) and Team ($25/user/month) plans.

## Lesson

Claude Projects are persistent workspaces on Pro ($20/month) and Team ($25/user/month) plans. Core capabilities include persistent custom instructions shaping every conversation; uploaded knowledge documents (PDFs, Word, spreadsheets) always available; isolated Project Memory separate from global Chat Memory; and team sharing with shared context and access management.

Setup: Click "Projects" in sidebar → "+ New Project" → name it → add custom instructions → upload knowledge files. Organization strategies include by client (agencies), by campaign type (in-house teams), and by product line (multi-product companies).
