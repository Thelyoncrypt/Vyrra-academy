# MCP Security: Sandboxing, Permissions, and Auditing

> OWASP ASI04 (Agentic Supply Chain) identifies compromised MCP servers as a critical risk vector.

## Lesson

OWASP ASI04 (Agentic Supply Chain) identifies compromised MCP servers as a critical risk vector. The Trivy-to-LiteLLM supply chain attack (February-March 2026) demonstrated how an infected binary can steal credentials and compromise the entire agent ecosystem.
