## 13.1 AI Product Discovery

**Key Concepts:**

- **Problem-first approach**: Find problems worth solving

- **AI feasibility assessment**: Can AI actually solve this?

- **Competitive analysis**: What's already in the market?

- **MVP definition**: Minimum viable AI product

- **Validation techniques**: Landing pages, waitlists, prototypes

### AI Product Discovery Framework

```text
Step 1: Problem Identification
  → What repetitive tasks do people complain about?
  → Where is human error costly?
  → What requires expertise that's expensive to hire?

Step 2: AI Feasibility Check
  → Is the problem pattern-based? (Good for AI)
  → Is there existing training data?
  → Can errors be tolerated or easily corrected?

Step 3: Solution Sketch
  → What's the simplest AI-assisted workflow?
  → What would the user experience be?
  → What are the technical requirements?

Step 4: Validate
  → Build a prototype in 1-2 days
  → Get feedback from 5-10 potential users
  → Iterate based on feedback
```

**Key Concepts:**

- **Custom assistants**: Tailored to specific domains/workflows

- **System prompts**: Define assistant personality and rules

- **Knowledge bases**: Ground responses in domain-specific data

- **Tool integration**: Connect to APIs and databases

- **Deployment options**: Web, Slack, Discord, WhatsApp, Telegram

### Building a Customer Support Assistant

```python
# Architecture
# 1. Knowledge base: Product docs, FAQs, past tickets
# 2. Tools: Order lookup, refund processing, escalation
# 3. Guardrails: Only answer support questions, escalate complex issues
# 4. Memory: Remember conversation context
# 5. Handoff: Transfer to human when needed

system_prompt = """
You are a customer support assistant for [Company].
Your role is to help customers with:
- Order status inquiries
- Product questions
- Return and refund requests
- Technical troubleshooting

Rules:
- Always be polite and professional
- Never share customer data with other customers
- Escalate to human agents for:
  * Angry or frustrated customers
  * Legal or compliance questions
  * Issues requiring manager approval
- Use the order_lookup tool for order questions
- Use the refund_tool for refund requests (up to $100)
"""
```

## 13.4 ROI Measurement

**Key Concepts:**

- **Time saved**: Hours automated × hourly cost

- **Error reduction**: Cost of errors before vs. after

- **Scale**: Volume of work processed

- **Quality**: Consistency and accuracy improvements

- **Employee satisfaction**: Reduction in tedious work

### ROI Calculation Template

```text
Before AI:
- Task time: 10 hours/week
- Hourly rate: $50
- Weekly cost: $500
- Error rate: 5% (cost: $200/week in fixes)
- Total weekly cost: $700

After AI:
- Task time: 2 hours/week (human oversight)
- AI cost: $100/week
- Error rate: 1% (cost: $40/week)
- Total weekly cost: $240

Weekly savings: $700 - $240 = $460
Annual savings: $460 × 52 = $23,920
ROI: ($23,920 - implementation_cost) / implementation_cost × 100
```
