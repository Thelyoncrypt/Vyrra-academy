## 11.1 GPT Image 2, Midjourney v7, FLUX 2

**Key Concepts:**

### GPT Image 2 (OpenAI)

- Integrated into ChatGPT and Codex

- Excellent text rendering in images

- Good for: Diagrams, UI mockups, illustrations

- Commercial use: Allowed with subscription

### Midjourney v7

- Best-in-class artistic quality

- Strong style consistency

- Discord-based interface

- Commercial use: Available with Pro plan

- Best for: Concept art, marketing visuals, creative projects

### FLUX 2 (Black Forest Labs)

- Open-weights model (self-hostable)

- High fidelity and prompt adherence

- Multiple variants: FLUX.2 [dev], [pro], [schnell]

- Commercial use: Varies by variant

- Best for: Technical accuracy, customization

### Image Prompting Best Practices

```text
Structure: [Subject], [Style], [Lighting], [Composition], [Details]

Example: "A modern coffee shop interior, warm golden hour lighting,
architectural photography style, wide-angle composition,
exposed brick walls, plants hanging from ceiling,
customers working on laptops, shallow depth of field"

Negative prompts: "blurry, low quality, distorted, ugly"
```

## 11.5 Commercial Licensing & Copyright

**Key Concepts:**

- **AI-generated content copyright**: Varies by jurisdiction

- **US**: AI-generated content is not copyrightable without human authorship

- **EU**: More nuanced, case-by-case basis

- **Commercial use**: Most tools allow commercial use with paid plans

- **Responsibility**: You are responsible for generated content

- **Watermarking**: Some tools embed invisible watermarks

### Best Practices

1. **Always review AI-generated content** before publishing

2. **Add human creative input** to strengthen copyright claims

3. **Document your process** (prompts, iterations, edits)

4. **Check tool-specific terms**: Each platform has different rules

5. **Disclose AI usage** when required (some jurisdictions)

6. **Avoid generating** trademarked characters, logos, or real people

### Prompt Engineering for Images

Write 5 prompts for the same subject with different styles:

```text
Subject: A futuristic city

1. Photorealistic: "A futuristic city skyline at dusk, photorealistic,
   8K, shot on Sony A7IV, 24mm lens, dramatic clouds, neon lights
   reflecting on wet streets"

2. Anime: "A futuristic city in anime style, Studio Ghibli inspired,
   hand-drawn aesthetic, vibrant colors, flying vehicles,
   cherry blossom trees, warm lighting"

3. Watercolor: "A futuristic city, watercolor painting style,
   soft edges, pastel colors, impressionistic, dreamy atmosphere"

4. Cyberpunk: "A cyberpunk city, neon-soaked streets, rain,
   holographic advertisements, dystopian atmosphere,
   Blade Runner style, cinematic"

5. Minimalist: "A futuristic city, minimalist design,
   clean lines, white and gold color palette,
   geometric shapes, architectural visualization"
```
